SMART URBAN FARMING EXPO - SUFex
"India's only Trade Fair specialized on Urban Farming" Smart Urban Farming Expo will be organized by SUFex from 24-25, March 2023 At Thayagaraj Stadium, INA, New Delhi, India Today, around the world, people are starting to grow food in the most untraditional places and spaces. Urban farming has become the new frontier in farming. From simple community vegetable gardens to providing healthy food to consumers in the nearby areas, with smart technology, urban farming can go anywhere. In metropolitan cities, where sufficient space is a luxury, urban farming can be a boon for the citizens and common masses. Lately, health concerns have further gravitated the focus of citizens at large for food safety, healthy environment and aesthetic surroundings. Urban Farming is the fastest growing industry within the farming industry with specific requirements not catered to by the normal farming shows. This leaves room for a show catering to this new group of farmers who grow their products in the middle of a city, on a balcony or a rooftop. SUFex aims to showcase the Technologies, Products, Services , Innovations and Designs of Urban Farming ,and will bring together all stakeholders of Urban Farming particularly for, Vertical Farming, Indoor Farming, Bee Farming, Landscaping and Gardening, Biofloc Fish Farming etc.
    Sessions/Topics
  • Hydroponics
  • Aeroponics
  • Aquaponics
  • Smart Landscaping and Gardening
  • Consultants/Architects/Contractors
  • Terrace Gardening
  • Organic Farms
  • Furniture and Lights
Date: 24-25, March 2023
Venue: At Thayagaraj Stadium, INA, New Delhi, India
Website: https://smarturbanfarmingexpo.com/
Anna Expo 2023
Anna Expo 2023 is an initiative by Annasaheb Shinde Foundation. The year also happens to be the birth centenary of Annasaheb Shinde, also known as the Father of India's Food Security. The Expo is scheduled from 5th to 9th April 2023 in Pune. Agriculture is the largest sector in India with 58% population having farming as the main source of profession. In the last few years, it has been a focus industry for the government of India. The Anna Expo 2023 offers exponential potential for setting up new technologies and innovations. The expo also tries to address bigger challenges of introducing Indian farmers to new age farming technologies, equipment's as well as various policies & practices by authorities and Government bodies. This expo is launched with a mission for the benefit of Indian agriculture by connecting available technology and resources directly to the farmer. MISSION To facilitate the fragmented land holding Farmers & Female Self Help Groups to stand on their own capacity. OBJECTIVE Through the regionally available resources we strengthen the female self help groups and facilitate them as a producer company with proper training, technology, technical support, finance & marketing.

  • Machinery & Equipments
  • Vertical Farming
Date : 5-9 April 2023

Vibrant North East 2023
